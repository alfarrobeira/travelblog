Found my place to be

by Anja, 31.01.2020

First time on the island. I arrived yesterday. Today I am going on my first hike. And what can I tell you? I fell in love with the island right away. Came as a complete surprise. I hadn't expected anything, just wanted to spend my remaining vacation days anyplace warmer than in Germany and a friend had told me, Madeira was good for hiking.
So here I am, standing at the north-eastern most part of Madeira, at the Ponta de São Lourenço. Looking at this beautiful landscape, the cliffs, the ocean, feeling the fresh breeze, breathing deeply and suddenly thinking: This is the perfect place: combination of sea and mountains. This is where I want to be for retirement. Maybe earlier...
